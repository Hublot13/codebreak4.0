CodeBreak 4.0

CodeBreak 4.0 is one of the most-awaited innovation challenges jointly-organised by Department of Computer Science Engineering & Department of Information Technology, MIT ADT University.
The action oriented learning and competitive environment for all participants is the reason why CodeBreak has experienced exponential growth in terms of reach and participation, along the way through each edition.
